Lumenfall

A year ago the Greydawn came, and the world began to drain of color. Flowers ash over; the sky dims; and those whose color runs out become the Faded — hollow, restful, gone. You are Wren, a dyer’s apprentice. When old Sable fades before your eyes, you set out to find the three Prisms and carry them to the Wellspring, where color is born.
